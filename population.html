World Population Density (people/km²)

Population density map of the world showing countries and subdivisions.

Population density is a measurement of population per unit area, or unit volume; it is a quantity of type number density. It is frequently applied to living organisms, most of the time to humans. It is a key geographical term. In simple terms, population density refers to the number of people living in an area per square kilometre.
